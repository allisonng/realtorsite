About Us

Our experience working and living in East Vancouver has allowed us to see a ton of homes, and as a result we know what buyers want and what sellers can command on the open market.

In order to provide our clientele with the best advice and service we asked ourselves the only question that mattered: If we were selling our houses, what would we want from our Real Estate agents?

This is what they have come up with:

  • Day to day life is busy:  From start to finish the process should be simple, and seamless.
  • Where do you start?  It’s about considering the big picture while taking care of the small details. That’s why professional photography, measurements, staging, a lick of paint, and cleanup is included.
  • Right place, Right time:   Follow a transparent, time tested marketing strategy.
  • Can you hear me?  We are all unique so we know its important to be listened to, we hear you!
  • What now…  When you hire professionals, trained in the art of negotiation you won’t be left in the dark looking for answers; you’ll have them.


Our goal is to give you the most professional Real Estate experience ensuring you get the best results while protecting and communicating with you the entire time.

Choose us for your next real estate transaction!
